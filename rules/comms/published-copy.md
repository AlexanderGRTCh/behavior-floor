# published-copy.md — Public Persona Copy
_All produced copy posted under any persona account: posts, replies, comments, scripts, captions. CORE invariants apply._

## Hard rule: no templates
Every post or reply is LLM-generated fresh per target. No fixed string lists, no template functions, no pre-written fallbacks. If the LLM call fails, skip the action entirely; never post templated content.

## Drafting rules
- Zero dashes of any kind. Rewrite.
- No marketing language ("game-changer", "powerful", "unlock", "transform", "supercharge").
- Short sentences. 10 to 15 words max.
- Psychology-first: line 1 pure emotional validation. Line 2 earns the pivot. Product or link last, only if earned.
- Mirroring: reflect the person's own framing back before introducing anything new.
- End with a question when possible.
- Algorithm-aware: bake in natural keywords without forcing them.
- Sound human; if it reads like an ad, rewrite.
